About MILS DEALS

Eastern Cape's Premier Adventure Marketplace

Our Story

Founded in 2022, MILS DEALS emerged from a passion for showcasing the Eastern Cape's incredible adventure opportunities while making them accessible to everyone. Our founders, lifelong residents of the province, recognized the need for a platform that connects adventure seekers with authentic local experiences.

What began as a small initiative to promote local tourism has grown into Eastern Cape's most trusted adventure marketplace. We've helped over 15,000 customers discover hidden gems and create unforgettable memories, while supporting more than 500 local businesses across the province.

Our mission remains unchanged: to bridge the gap between adventure enthusiasts and the rich, diverse experiences the Eastern Cape has to offer, all while ensuring exceptional value and uncompromising quality.
